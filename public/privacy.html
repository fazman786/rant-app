PRIVACY POLICY · LAST UPDATED MAY 2026

RANT ("we", "us", or "our") is an anonymous social venting app available at rantit.app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Username — chosen by you at signup. This is public.
  • Email address — optional, used only for password recovery.
  • Rants and replies — text and voice content you post publicly.
  • Reactions and follows — interactions with other users' content.
  • Voice recordings — stored securely in our cloud storage.
  • Usage data — streaks, coins, and activity within the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the RANT service
  • To send password reset emails (only if you provided an email)
  • To display your public rants and profile to other users
  • To calculate streaks, coins, and heat scores

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. Voice notes are stored in Supabase Storage. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Anonymous by Default

RANT is designed to be anonymous. Your real name is never required. You choose your own username. Email is optional and never shown publicly.

5. Third Party Services

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI features (rant validation, category detection)
  • Resend — email delivery for password resets
  • Vercel — app hosting

6. Data Retention

Your account and rants remain until you delete them. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing us.

7. Children's Privacy

RANT is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

  • Access your data at any time through the app
  • Delete your account and data
  • Update your username or email
  • Request a copy of your data

9. Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact us at: privacy@rantit.app

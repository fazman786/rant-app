CHILD SAFETY POLICY · LAST UPDATED MAY 2026

RANT ("the app") is committed to the safety and protection of children. This policy outlines our standards against child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and related harmful content.

1. Zero Tolerance Policy

RANT has a strict zero-tolerance policy toward any content that sexually exploits, abuses, or endangers minors. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) of any kind
  • Content that sexualises minors
  • Content that facilitates grooming, trafficking, or exploitation of children
  • Any content that endangers the safety or wellbeing of minors

2. Age Requirements

RANT is intended for users aged 13 and over. Users under 13 are not permitted to create accounts or use the app. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

3. Content Reporting

All users can report content that violates our policies using the in-app report feature (available on every rant). Reports are reviewed and action is taken promptly.

If you encounter content that involves child sexual abuse or exploitation, please report it immediately:

  • Using the in-app report button
  • By emailing us at safety@rantit.app
  • By reporting to your national authority (e.g. NCMEC in the US, IWF in the UK)

4. Enforcement

Any account found to be posting, sharing, or promoting CSAE content will be:

  • Immediately suspended
  • Reported to relevant law enforcement authorities
  • Permanently banned from the platform

5. Cooperation with Authorities

RANT will cooperate fully with law enforcement agencies investigating child exploitation or abuse, including providing account information and content where legally required.

6. Contact

To report child safety concerns: safety@rantit.app
